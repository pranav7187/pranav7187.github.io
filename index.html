Pranav Bakshi

I am a fourth year dual degree student (B.Tech + M.Tech) studying Mechanical Engineering at Indian Institute of Technology Kharagpur (IIT KGP). My interests revolve around Robotics, Controls, Reinforcement Learning and Computer Vision. I am especially fascinated by complex underactuated systems.

Currently, I am working as a Research Intern at IDEAS Lab, Purdue University on motion planning problems using reinforcement learning. I am also pursuing my Bachelor's Thesis Project (BTP) under the supervision of Prof. Bhargav Jha working on herding problem using controls.

Previously, I have worked as a Research Intern at FOCAS Lab, RBCCPS, IISc Bangalore under the supervision of Prof. Pushpak Jagtap working on safety-critical controls.
